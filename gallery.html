Travel Photography Tips

Golden Hour Magic

Shoot during the first hour after sunrise or the last hour before sunset for warm, soft lighting that enhances any landscape or portrait.

Connect with Locals

Always ask permission before photographing people. A smile and respect will often lead to authentic portraits and cultural exchanges.

Phone Photography

You don't need expensive gear! Modern smartphones can capture amazing photos. Focus on composition and lighting rather than equipment.
