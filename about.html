My Journey From the Philippines to the World

A story of transformation, cultural connections, and finding purpose through travel and storytelling.

The Beginning of My Journey

I was born in Dublin,Ireland, but I was raised in the vibrant landscapes of the Philippines. I can remember the tropical's sun's warmth, monsoon rains, and uniquely Filipino culture even in my early childhood and it surely did influenced the way I viewed the world.

My journey began in a small coastal town where life moved at its own pace. I helped my family at their small business while also going to school, and imagining my future. Even as a child, I was curious about the world beyond our shores, inspired by stories my grandmother would tell of distant lands and different ways of life.

At 15, my life took an unexpected turn when my family relocated to Dublin, Ireland. The transition from a tropical paradise to the emerald landscapes of Ireland was both challenging and transformative. Learning to navigate a new culture, climate, and educational system taught me resilience and adaptability – skills that would become the foundation of my future travels.

Those early years shaped my identity as a global citizen, with roots in the Philippines and branches extending across continents. The blend of Eastern and Western perspectives gave me a unique lens through which to view the world – one that celebrates diversity while recognizing our shared humanity.

My Path

Life Timeline

2003

Born in Dublin, Ireland

Though born in Dublin, my early childhood was spent in the beautiful province of Ilocos sur in the Philippines.

2018

Moved to Dublin, Ireland

My family relocated to Dublin, introducing me to a completely different culture and way of life.

2023

First Solo Trip: Backpacking Europe

After saving for two years, I embarked on my first solo adventure across Western Europe, visiting 7 countries in 3 months.

2022

Started This Blog

During the global pandemic, I started sharing my travel stories online, connecting with fellow travelers worldwide.

2022

Return to the Philippines

Returned to my homeland with fresh eyes, rediscovering my roots and documenting the beauty of Filipino culture.

2025

Present Day: Global Nomad

Today, I split my time between Ireland, the Philippines, and wherever my adventures take me next.

My commitment is to share both the beauty and challenges of travel, presenting realistic experiences rather than just picture-perfect moments.
