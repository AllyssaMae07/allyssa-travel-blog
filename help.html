Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on the "Account" button in the top navigation bar and select "Register." Fill out the required information and submit the form. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

After creating an account, visit the "Community" page and click on "Write For Us." You'll find guidelines for submissions and a form to submit your travel story for review.

On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to reset your password. For security reasons, the link will expire after 24 hours.

Yes, you can change your username in your account settings. Go to your profile, click on "Settings," then navigate to the "Account" tab to update your username.
